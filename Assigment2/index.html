Major Earthquakes from 1965 - 2016

Earthquakes are the shaking of the earth’s surface caused by a sudden release of energy, creating seismic waves. Earthquakes can be so small humans can’t detect them, so violent they can destroy cities or anything in between. They strength of the quake is measured in magnitude, which is a scale from 1 to 9. A magnitude of 5.3 is a moderate earthquake, while 6.3 is strong. The scale is logarithmic, meaning each 1 unit increase represents a tenfold increase in strength.
